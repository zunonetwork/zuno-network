# Zuno Network

**Improved AI Accuracy, Powered by People and Decentralized Compute**

**The Problem:** AI is smart, but it frequently makes mistakes.

AI often gives answers that look correct but aren't. 
These "hallucinations" create real risks in healthcare, 
legal, finance, and everyday decisions. Fixing them is 
expensive and controlled by a handful of large companies 
— leaving everyone else out of the process.

**The Solution:** Zuno Network

Zuno Network uses a smartphone app (Android and iOS) 
that turns everyday people into AI trainers. By reviewing 
AI-generated answers — a process the industry calls 
**data labeling** — contributors help improve AI accuracy 
and earn **Zuno tokens** on the Solana blockchain as a 
reward for every task completed.

No experience needed. No cost to join. 
Anyone in the world can contribute and earn.

*Zuno tokens are earned as rewards for completing tasks 
and are not offered as a financial investment.*
---

## Why Zuno

- **Human-validated AI data** — RLHF App Workers review AI-generated Q&A pairs via the Zuno mobile app
- **Decentralized compute** — GPU Workers run the Zuno container from home and earn for inference tasks
- **Fair-launch token** — No presale, no private investors, no team tokens. Same entry price for everyone
- **Built on Solana** — Fast, transparent, low-cost reward distribution
- **Accuracy at scale** — Consensus-verified data trains more reliable AI over time

---

## How It Works

1. **Distribute** — AI training tasks are broken into small pieces and sent to the Zuno app
2. **Validate** — RLHF App Workers pick the most accurate AI response for each task
3. **Compute** — GPU Workers provide inference power and are rewarded for uptime and reliability
4. **Reward** — Completed tasks earn points (pre-mine) or ZUNO tokens (mainnet) from the Community Treasury
5. **Improve** — Verified outputs feed back into AI training, raising accuracy over time

## Current Phase

**Phase 0 — Private Beta Testing**
The Android app is being tested privately. RLHF App Worker workflows and the Zuno API task pipeline are being validated before the public pre-mine begins. During the pre-mine, contributors earn points (not tokens) — 1 task = 1 point, max 10 per day. Points convert to ZUNO at 1:1 via airdrop at TGE.
