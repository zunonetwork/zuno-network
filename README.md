# Zuno Network

**Improved AI Accuracy, Powered by People and Decentralized Compute**

AI is getting smarter — but it still gets things wrong. Hallucinations create real liability across healthcare, finance, compliance, and beyond. Fixing this today means paying a handful of large companies to control the process.

Zuno Network opens that up. Anyone with an Android phone can review AI outputs and earn **ZUNO tokens** on Solana. Anyone with a GPU can contribute compute. Every token in circulation is earned through real work — no presales, no insiders, no team allocations.

---

## Why Zuno

- **Human-validated AI data** — RLHF App Workers review AI-generated Q&A pairs via the Zuno mobile app
- **Decentralized compute** — GPU Workers run the Zuno container from home and earn for inference tasks
- **Fair-launch token** — No presale, no private investors, no team tokens. Same entry price for everyone
- **Built on Solana** — Fast, transparent, low-cost reward distribution
- **Accuracy at scale** — Consensus-verified data trains more reliable AI over time

---

## How It Works

1. **Distribute** — AI training tasks are broken into small pieces and sent to the Zuno app
2. **Validate** — RLHF App Workers pick the most accurate AI response for each task
3. **Compute** — GPU Workers provide inference power and are rewarded for uptime and reliability
4. **Reward** — Completed tasks release ZUNO from the Community Treasury to contributors
5. **Improve** — Verified outputs feed back into AI training, raising accuracy over time
