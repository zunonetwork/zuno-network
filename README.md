# Zuno Network

**Improved AI Accuracy, Powered by Humans**

**The Problem:** AI is a powerful tool — but it still makes mistakes.

AI hallucinations produce incorrect answers with confidence, creating real‑world risk for businesses, governments, and users who need accuracy.


**The Solution:** Zuno Network

Zuno Network uses a smartphone app (Android and iOS) 
that turns everyday people into AI trainers. By reviewing 
AI-generated answers — a process the industry calls 
**data labeling** — contributors help improve AI accuracy 
and earn **Zuno tokens** on the Solana blockchain as a 
reward for every task completed.

No experience needed. No cost to join. 
Anyone in the world can contribute and earn.

*Zuno tokens are earned as rewards for completing tasks 
and are not offered as a financial investment.*

---

## Why Zuno

- **Human-validated AI data** — Contributors review AI-generated 
  answers via the Zuno app
- **Community-driven** — Every token is earned through real work, 
  not purchased or allocated in advance
- **Built on Solana** — Fast, transparent, low-cost reward distribution
- **Accuracy at scale** — Consensus-verified data trains more 
  reliable AI over time

---

## How It Works

1. **Distribute** — AI training tasks are broken into small pieces 
   and sent to the Zuno app
2. **Label** — Contributors select the most accurate AI response 
   for each task
3. **Reward** — Completed tasks earn points (pre-mine) or ZUNO 
   tokens (at launch) from the Community Treasury
4. **Improve** — Verified outputs feed back into AI training, 
   raising accuracy over time

## Current Phase

**Phase 0 — Private Beta Testing**
The Zuno app is being tested privately. Contributor workflows 
and the task pipeline are being validated before the public 
pre-mine begins. During the pre-mine, contributors earn points 
(not tokens) — 1 task = 1 point, max 10 per day. Points convert 
to ZUNO at 1:1 via airdrop at launch.
